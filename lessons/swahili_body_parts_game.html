Habari! Before we play, let's learn the words first. 🎉

Swahili is phonetic — every letter is pronounced! So Kichwa = "Kee-chwa", Mguu = "M-goo", Mkono = "M-ko-no". Say each word out loud as you study!
Notice the pattern: many body parts start with M- (like Mkono, Mguu, Mdomo, Moyo). In Swahili, the M- prefix often marks a body noun. Cool, right?
